About Me

I am a Lecturer in Embedded and Intelligent Systems at the University of Essex and lead the Internet of Things and Machine Intelligence Lab. My research explores IoT, Edge Computing, ML, Signal Processing, and Explainable AI in healthcare and intelligent systems. I am a member of the EPSRC Peer Review College, a registered EC expert, and serve as a reviewer for leading journals including IEEE IoT Journal and PLOS ONE.

Research Interests

  • Internet of Things in Healthcare
  • Machine Learning, Signal Processing
  • Time Series Analysis, Semantic Web
  • Multimodal & Multi-aspect Learning
  • Explainable & Energy-Efficient AI
  • High-order Tensor Decompositions
  • Edge & Federated Learning

Selected Projects

Smart Glasses for Blind Navigation

Multimodal navigation system combining vision, audio, and gait data with AI on the edge.

Remote Depression Monitoring

AI-driven wearable and app-based framework for continuous mental health tracking.

Respiratory Monitoring

Low-cost acoustic sensing system for respiratory anomalies using signal processing and deep learning.
